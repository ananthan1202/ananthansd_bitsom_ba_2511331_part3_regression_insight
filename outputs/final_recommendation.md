# Final Recommendation

## Best Model

The Multiple Linear Regression model is recommended because it explains approximately 81.14% of the variation in monthly sales compared to only 16.72% explained by the Simple Linear Regression model.

## Key Findings

- Marketing Spend has a positive and statistically significant impact on sales.
- Footfall is one of the strongest predictors of monthly sales.
- Inventory Availability significantly improves sales performance.
- Regional differences also influence sales, with some regions performing significantly better than others.

## Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing investments where returns are highest.
- Improve customer footfall through promotions and campaigns.
- Maintain high inventory availability to prevent stock-outs.
- Allocate additional resources to high-performing regions while investigating weaker regions.

## Conclusion

The Multiple Regression model provides substantially better prediction accuracy and should be used for future business planning and sales forecasting.
